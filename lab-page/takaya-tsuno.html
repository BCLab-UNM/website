Takaya Tsuno

tsuno@robot.mach.mie-u.ac.jp

Takaya Tsuno is a postgraduate in Mechanical Engineering at Mie University in Japan. He currently conducts research on the development of a cleaning robot which can perform dangerous tasks as replacement of workers. His mentor at the Mechatronics Laboratory at Mie University is Professor Ken'ichi Yano.
